### Project Overview

**Fuel Watch** is a mobile application designed to help users track fuel consumption, optimize driving habits, and save on fuel costs. With its simple and intuitive design, the app helps users accurately monitor the amount of fuel they fill up, how long it lasts, and how their vehicles perform in terms of fuel efficiency. The app uses historical driving data to provide users with tips on how to improve driving economy and save money in the long term.

### Application Content

* **Home Page**: Displays an overview of fuel consumption with charts and statistics on driving efficiency.
* **Fuel Logs**: Allows users to enter all data related to fuel filling (amount, price, date).
* **Record Tracking**: Based on the entered data, the app provides users with recommendations for improving driving efficiency.
* **Support**: Provides access to FAQs and customer support.

### Usage

Once the app is up and running, you can use the following features:

1. **Sign Up and Login**: Create a user account or log in to start tracking your fuel consumption.
2. **Fuel Entry**: Manually enter the amount of fuel, price, and date of filling.
3. **View Statistics**: View all fuel entries and driving efficiency analysis.
